## WhatsApp Business Communications

This addendum supplements our main Privacy Policy and specifically addresses how Rillcod Technologies collects, uses, and protects your information when you communicate with us via WhatsApp Business.

### 1. Information We Collect via WhatsApp

When you communicate with us through WhatsApp, we collect:

**Automatically Collected:**
- Your phone number
- Message content (text, images, documents, videos you send)
- Message timestamps
- Message delivery and read status
- Device information (provided by WhatsApp)

**Voluntarily Provided:**
- Your name (if you provide it)
- Student information
- School affiliation
- Payment inquiries
- Support requests
- Any other information you choose to share

### 2. How We Use Your WhatsApp Data

We use information collected via WhatsApp to:

- **Respond to your inquiries** - Answer questions about courses, payments, assignments
- **Provide customer support** - Resolve technical issues and concerns
- **Send notifications** - Assignment reminders, payment confirmations, class schedules (only if you opt-in)
- **Link your account** - Connect your WhatsApp number to your Rillcod portal account
- **Improve our service** - Analyze common questions to improve our platform
- **Comply with legal obligations** - Maintain records as required by law

### 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your WhatsApp data based on:

- **Consent** - You initiate contact with us via WhatsApp
- **Contract Performance** - To provide services you've requested
- **Legitimate Interests** - To respond to inquiries and provide support
- **Legal Obligation** - To maintain records as required by Nigerian law

### 4. Your Consent and Opt-In

**Explicit Consent Required:**
- We will only send you proactive WhatsApp messages if you explicitly opt-in
- Opt-in can be provided during registration or by messaging us first
- You can withdraw consent at any time by replying "STOP"

**What Happens When You Opt-In:**
- You'll receive important updates about your account
- Assignment and exam reminders
- Payment confirmations
- School announcements
- Support responses

**What We Won't Do:**
- Send marketing messages without consent
- Share your number with third parties
- Spam you with unnecessary messages
- Send messages after you opt-out

### 5. Opt-Out Rights

**You can opt-out anytime by:**
1. Replying "STOP" to any WhatsApp message from us
2. Visiting your dashboard settings and disabling WhatsApp notifications
3. Emailing privacy@rillcod.com with your opt-out request

**What Happens When You Opt-Out:**
- You'll receive a confirmation message
- We'll stop sending you automated notifications
- You can still message us for support
- We'll still respond to your inquiries
- You can opt back in anytime by replying "START"

### 6. Data Storage and Security

**Where Your Data is Stored:**
- WhatsApp messages are stored on our secure servers (Supabase)
- Hosted in secure data centers with encryption
- Access restricted to authorized staff only

**Security Measures:**
- End-to-end encryption (provided by WhatsApp)
- Secure database with role-based access control
- Regular security audits
- Staff training on data protection
- Two-factor authentication for admin access

**Data Retention:**
- Active conversations: Retained while your account is active
- Inactive conversations: Deleted after 90 days of inactivity
- Opted-out users: Contact information retained for 30 days, then deleted
- You can request immediate deletion at any time

### 7. Third-Party Services

**WhatsApp Business API (Meta Platforms):**
- We use Meta's WhatsApp Business API to send and receive messages
- Meta processes your phone number and message content
- Meta's Privacy Policy applies: https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy
- Meta may use metadata for their own purposes (not message content)

**Our Hosting Provider (Supabase):**
- Messages are stored on Supabase servers
- Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit

**We Do Not:**
- Sell your WhatsApp data to third parties
- Share your number with marketers
- Use your messages for advertising
- Allow unauthorized access to your conversations

**Object** - Object to processing based on legitimate interests
